Located in the Dallas Design District, Caldwell Arte Exposicion is a Gallery that presents unique and inspiring collections, displaying a variety of abstract and contemporary pieces.

The gallery features seven exhibitions per year, showcasing unique and inspiring pieces from established mid-career and emerging contemporary artists.

With a diverse look from one style to the next, the gallery appeals to a wide range of sensorium to the art enthusiast.

Caldwell Arte Exposicion invites collectors, designers, and art enthusiests alike, to visit and experience the thoughts and emotions brought on by unadulterated artistic expression.
